LinkedIn™ Learning

Courses

LinkedIn™ gives access to thousands of courses both free and paid, with courses available in almost every field imaginable from business to arts to writing and even sales.
Courses include all of the following:
  • Project files
  • Chapter quizzes
  • Certificate of completion


Learning Paths

LinkedIn™ offers its users the opportunity to specialize in a particular field. This is achived by creating Learning Paths for the user. The learning paths act as guides. They are clusters of courses that the user must complete in order to gain a certain level of expertise in their preferred field.
One great benefit to the learning paths is that it is benefial regardless of the user's expertise. A beginner and an expert could complete a learning path and both will gain knowledge from it.
